About

I am currently pursuing the Ph.D degree with Science and Technology of Remote Sensing in the School of Remote Sensing and Information Engineering from Wuhan University, supervised by Prof. Jianya Gong and Prof. Shunping Ji. I received my B.S. degree in Science and Technology of Remote Sensing from Wuhan University in 2018, Hubei, China.
My research focuses on deep learning based multi-view stereo, 3D reconstruction, and large-scale urban reconstruction with UAV and optical satellite imagery.
